About Dane

A Voice for
Alberta in Ottawa

Dane Lloyd grew up in Parkland riding — his family has farmed outside of Spruce Grove for generations. He is a proud life-long Albertan, serving alongside his wife Raechel and their three children: Brooke, Alexander, and Harrison.

First elected in October 2017, re-elected in 2019, 2021, and again in April 2025, Dane is one of the most trusted Conservative MPs in Canada. As Shadow Minister for Emergency Preparedness, he brings real strength and conviction to every fight in Ottawa.

What Dane Fights For

Priorities That Matter

Emergency Preparedness

As Shadow Minister, Dane pressed the Liberal government after the devastating 2024 Jasper wildfire, exposing years of failure to protect vulnerable communities.

Public Safety

Sitting on the Standing Committee on Public Safety and National Security, Dane fights against the Liberal gun confiscation regime and foreign election interference.

Alberta Communities

From Spruce Grove's growing families to Drayton Valley's energy workers, Dane advocates relentlessly for every corner of Parkland riding.

Energy & Economy

Defending Alberta's energy sector and the livelihoods of thousands of Parkland families who depend on a strong, responsible resource economy.

Rural Families

Championing the values of rural Alberta — from farm policy to mental health services for our agricultural communities across the riding.

Cybersecurity & Democracy

A leading voice on Canada's cybersecurity legislation and foreign interference — protecting the integrity of Canadian elections and institutions.
