Common Questions

How quickly will I hear back?

Dane's office aims to respond within 1–2 business days. Complex case work may take longer.

Can Dane help with passport or immigration issues?

Yes — as your MP, Dane's office can contact federal departments on your behalf to help resolve delays or issues.

How do I invite Dane to a community event?

Use the contact form above or call the constituency office. Please provide as much advance notice as possible.

Do I need to live in Parkland riding?

Dane's office primarily serves constituents of Parkland riding. If you live elsewhere, please contact your own MP.
