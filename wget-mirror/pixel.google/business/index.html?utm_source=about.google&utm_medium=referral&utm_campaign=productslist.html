Got questions? Start here first.

How easy is it to integrate Pixel with my current infrastructure?

Very easy. With Android Enterprise, Pixel supports Android zero-touch enrollment, so you can deploy corporate-owned devices in bulk without having to manually set up each one. Just open the box and Pixel is ready to go with everything you need.

Is it secure to use Pixel for my business?

With Pixel phones, you’ll have end-to-end security designed by Google to help give you and your business peace of mind. Pixel 10 phones and Pixel Tablet offer multi-layer hardware security, including the Titan M2TM security chip, anti-phishing and anti-malware protection, automatic Android security updates from 5 to 7 years, and more. 

Why should I switch to Pixel for my business?

Pixel 10 phones are designed with the tools and technology to support your business needs across hardware, software, and built-in Google AI. It offers the highest rated security features on a smartphone, including automatic OS and security updates for 7 years.  Pixel also comes with software to help you and your employees to perform more efficiently. And, you can easily integrate and manage it to ensure workflow is up to your standards at all times. Additionally, Pixel is part of the Android Enterprise Recommended program, meaning it has been verified by Google to meet enterprise-grade requirements for performance, consistency, and security updates.

I have a lot of employees. How can I manage all the devices?

With Android Enterprise, it’s easy to scale and support your mobile fleet. Standardized features let you manage devices seamlessly. Consistently every time. No matter how many you deploy.

How do I use the Gemini Mobile App on my Pixel? 

To get started:

  • Set up your Google Workspace account with enabled access to the Gemini Mobile App.

  • Make sure Gemini is set as your phone’s default assist app.

  • Long press the power button or open the Gemini Mobile App and start a conversation through typing, your voice, or images.

  • For more details, visit here.

Can a Pixel phone keep up with my employees and productivity?

Pixel 10 phones are thoughtfully engineered to be lightweight and portable for easy handling. An adaptive battery learns your favorite apps and reduces power to the ones you rarely use. This limits background battery usage and helps your phone last over 24 hours.  Also, regular security updates help keep the phone current and secure, while Pixel Drops add new functionality over time. 

What helpful features do Pixel phones come with?

Pixel is extremely easy to use because it comes with features that are designed to help. Your Pixel lets you find answers easily and quickly just by asking.  When it comes to your favorite services like Gmail, Meet, and support for your favorite third party apps it’s all already integrated right in.  Plus, Call Screen will help you screen unknown callers, robocalls, and spam calls, so you can focus on your work while minimizing interruption. 

Which Google Pixel phones have 5G?

Pixel 10 phones are built to support 5G that can supercharge the helpfulness of Google with an extra boost of speed.
