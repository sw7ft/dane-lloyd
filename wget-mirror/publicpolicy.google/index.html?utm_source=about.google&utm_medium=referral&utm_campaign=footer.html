OUR PUBLIC POLICY PRIORITIES

Learn more about our global public policy priorities

  • Supporting consumer choice

    Google’s work on data portability expands choice and supports an ecosystem of growth and innovation.

  • Enabling economic opportunity

    We help individuals, communities, businesses, and governments succeed in the digital economy

  • Protecting user privacy

    We support privacy regulation and policy that fosters trust and are committed to creating online experiences that are private-by-design, secure-by-default, and age-appropriate.

  • Advancing bold and responsible AI

    We aim to unlock the benefits of groundbreaking emerging technologies like AI to help solve society’s biggest challenges, while minimizing problems by developing safeguards and working with partners.

  • Strengthening security

    Cybersecurity is a global priority for policymakers. We are committed to helping achieve our collective goals of strengthening national security and the resilience of our infrastructure.

  • Energy and Sustainability

    We're leveraging the power of AI to help solve society’s biggest challenges and improve lives.

  • Protecting access to trustworthy information and content

    When our users place their trust in us, we feel a great responsibility to deliver trustworthy, helpful information that meets their needs. As a result, we are constantly evolving our efforts to combat harmful and illegal content.
