A new way to assist your customers. Anywhere, anytime.

Get started with the Google Assistant to help your customers find answers and get things done. Now available across more than 1B devices worldwide (and counting).

Be where your users are

The Google Assistant helps everyone get things done —in the home and on the go. Make sure you’re front and center when people are looking for your brand and services.

Give your brand a voice

Digital assistants are changing the way consumers interact with businesses. Build Actions for the Google Assistant to connect directly with your users through voice.

Drive real business results

The Google Assistant helps users buy products, book appointments, find stores, and more. Whatever your business does, the Google Assistant can help you grow metrics that matter.

New Actions added every day

Some of the worlds favorite brands have already created Actions for the Google Assistant. See what they are doing in our Actions directory.
