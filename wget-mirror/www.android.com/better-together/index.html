Get started with Quick Share

How to use Quick Share on Android.

1. Open content in any app
2. Tap "Share," then "Quick Share"
3. Select the device(s) to share with
4. Await acceptance from the recipient

Get started with Quick Share

How to use Quick Share on Windows.

1. Download and install the Quick Share app on your Windows PC
2. Sign in and enable "Quick Share" in your settings
3. Choose a file and select "Quick Share"
4. Select the device(s) to share with
5. Await acceptance from the recipient

Get started with Quick Share

How to use Quick Share on Chromebooks.

1. Enable "Quick Share" in your settings
2. Right-click on a file, then tap "Share"
3. Select the device to share with
4. Await acceptance from the recipient

Unknown tracker alerts

Stay safe on the go.

Your Android device can now automatically detect and notify you of unfamiliar tracking devices (like Bluetooth tracker tags) moving with you, so you can take action to locate and disable them.

Detection works with compatible trackers. Location Services and Bluetooth may need to be turned on. Requires cell service. Available on Android 6+ in all regions and languages Android supports.
