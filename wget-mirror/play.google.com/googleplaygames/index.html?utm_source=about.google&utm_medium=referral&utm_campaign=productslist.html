Frequently asked questions

What is Google Play Games?

Google Play Games elevates gaming on mobile, tablets, and PC, by making cross-device gameplay more seamless. The experience includes a singular gaming profile for mobile and PC, a large catalog of games playable across devices, rewards you can earn when gaming, organized gaming information, and the Play Games Sidekick with Gemini Live, a gaming companion that gives you easy access to information without leaving your game. The You tab and Sidekick are launching on mobile first.
How do I join Google Play Games?

To get started on mobile:
  1. Open the Google Play Store app on Android
  2. Tap your profile picture
  3. Tap “Join Google Play Games”
  4. Follow the steps to create a gamer profile.

You can also join Google Play Games through your PC:
  1. Download Google Play Games on your Windows desktop or laptop
  2. Open the .exe file and follow the on-screen instructions
  3. Setting up your account through Google Play Games on PC automatically sets up your Google Play Games profile. You’re all set.

For additional information, check out our Help Center article. Google Play Games on PC is available in more than 140 regions. Anyone in these regions with an eligible device can play on PC.
I have an iPhone. Can I still use Google Play Games?

Yes. Even without an Android mobile device, you can experience Google Play Games on your Windows PC device. Many games that are available on iOS mobile devices are also playable on Google Play Games on PC.
Can I set up multiple Play Games profiles?

You can only set up one gamer profile per Google Account. If you have multiple Google Accounts, you can create multiple gamer profiles.
Do I need to pay to use Google Play Games?

No, you do not have to pay to use Google Play Games on mobile or PC. However, when playing games, you may need to pay for a game or in-game items.
What games are available on PC versus on mobile?

We have a large selection of games across devices. Explore what’s available on mobile and PC.
How do I install games?

To install games on your Android mobile device, open the Google Play Store, search for a game, and tap “Install.” To install games on PC, after setting up Google Play Games on your PC, search for a game, and click “Install.”
If I don’t join Google Play Games, how is my experience different?

If you don’t join Google Play Games, you will not have access to certain features like the You tab or the Play Games Sidekick on your Android mobile device.
How do I delete my Play Games profile and data?

Learn how to delete your Play Games Profile here.
What are the minimum requirements to run Google Play Games on PC?

Your PC must meet these minimum requirements:
  • Windows 10 (v2004)
  • Solid state drive (SSD) with 10 GB of available storage space
  • IntelⓇ UHD Graphics 630 GPU or comparable
  • 4 CPU physical cores
  • 8 GB of RAM
  • Windows admin account
  • Hardware virtualization must be turned on
