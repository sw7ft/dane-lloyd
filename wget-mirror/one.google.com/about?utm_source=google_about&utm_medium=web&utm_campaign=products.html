One place to store all your things

More to store across your apps

More room for everything that you love. Google One gives you extra storage across Google Photos, Drive and Gmail – so your photos and videos are backed up, and your files and emails have the space that they need.

Auto device backups for peace of mind

Keep what matters most. Google One automatically backs up your photos, videos, contacts and more – so if you ever switch, break or lose your device, restoring everything is just a tap away.

More than enough room to share

Keep your crew covered. With Google One, you can share your storage with up to five others – at no extra cost. Everyone gets their own space, and your content remains private to you, even when you're sharing storage.

Frequently asked questions

Google Drive is a way to organise your files. Google One is a subscription that gives you more storage across Google Drive, Gmail and Google Photos.

You can back up data on your Android or iOS phone, and also clean up space in your Google Account using the storage manager. If you get a Google One plan, you'll unlock additional features like expanded cloud storage and member benefits.

Every Google Account comes with 15 GB of storage for Google Drive, Gmail and Google Photos. When you subscribe to Google One, your total storage increases to 100 GB or more depending on the plan you choose — which you can share with up to 5 other people.

Google Workspace Individual includes more premium features to help individual business owners run and grow their businesses. Google One is a consumer subscription plan that gives you more storage across Google Drive, Gmail and Google Photos, plus extra Google benefits and features. The Google One Premium plan comes with a limited number of Google Workspace Individual features, like longer group calls for Google Meet and enhanced appointment scheduling in Google Calendar.

For the Basic plan and above, you can share Google One with up to five other people at no extra cost (so six in total, including you). When you create a new family group, you can add or remove other family members. Certain benefits are only available to the plan manager. Family plan members on a Google AI Pro plan can enjoy AI benefits and features at no extra cost. Learn more.

If you're already a member of a family group on Google, you can share your Google One plan with the rest of your existing group.
