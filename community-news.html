PARKLAND ELECTORAL DISTRICT

Community News

Stay informed about what's happening across the 18 communities in the Parkland riding — from Spruce Grove and Stony Plain to Drayton Valley, Mayerthorpe, and beyond.

Spruce Grove & Stony Plain

Budget & Infrastructure

Stony Plain Council Approves 2025 Spring Budget

Council approved the 2025 Spring Budget Adjustment, reducing the tax increase from 7.41% to 6.71%, resulting in a $153,000 tax reduction for residents.

Community Project

Joint Trail Connection Nears Completion

Stony Plain and Spruce Grove announced the final phase of their Joint Trail Connection project, creating pathways between the two communities. Phase III involves paving the southern trail segment.

Municipal

New Public Works Building Opens

Stony Plain celebrated the opening of its new Public Works Administration Building, completed in just over a year. The facility serves as a critical hub for municipal operations.

Parkland County

Budget & Taxes

Lowest Tax Rates in Edmonton Region

Parkland County maintains tax rates amongst the lowest in the Edmonton region. The 2025 budget included a 2.5% municipal tax increase with strategic capital investments in roads, water infrastructure, and Wabamun projects.

Planning

New Municipal Development Plan Adopted

Following 60+ hours of community engagement across 20+ open houses, Parkland County adopted a new 30-year Municipal Development Plan establishing vision for growth, recreation, and environmental protection.

Land Use

Updated Land Use Bylaw

Parkland County adopted a new Land Use Bylaw after 90 hours of community engagement. The updated bylaw reflects the inclusion of Wabamun and consolidates districts for increased flexibility.

Drayton Valley & Brazeau County

Community Recognition

Mayor and Community Recognition Awards

Drayton Valley held its Mayor and Community Recognition Gala, honoring outstanding community members across nine categories including Arts and Music, Business Achievement, Community Spirit, and Excellence in Athletics.

Infrastructure

Provincial Funding for Stormwater Project

Brazeau County received $748,230 through the Drought and Flood Protection Program for the Hill Top Stormwater Improvement Project, which will divert stormwater to natural drainage outlets.

Hamlet of Cynthia

Bylaws Passed for Cynthia Development

Brazeau County Council passed bylaws for the Hamlet of Cynthia, including rezoning five county-owned lots from Urban Reserve to Hamlet Residential to support community growth.

Mayerthorpe & Lac Ste. Anne County

Commemoration

Fallen Four Memorial

Royal Canadian Air Force fighter jets conducted a flyover at the Fallen Four Memorial Park in Mayerthorpe to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the 2005 shooting that took the lives of four RCMP officers.

Regional Services

West End Bus Purchase

Lac Ste. Anne County joined Mayerthorpe and Woodlands County in purchasing a new 15-passenger West End Bus for $83,990 to serve seniors in the region. LSAC also funded dock repairs at Corsair Cove Boat Launch.

Election

2025 Council Election Results

In October 2025, all incumbent Lac Ste. Anne County councillors were defeated. In Division 6 (Mayerthorpe area), Jurgen Preugschas was elected, unseating incumbent Ross Bohnet.

Yellowhead County

Economic Development

Major Land Acquisition for Growth

Yellowhead County acquired approximately 1,850 acres of land on the county's west side as part of a long-term sustainable development strategy. The purchase, completed after over five years of collaboration with the Province of Alberta, aligns with plans to build a robust economy, enhance tourism, and support the Jasper/Hinton Airport as an economic driver.

Agriculture

Environmental Farm Plan Workshop

An Alberta Environmental Farm Plan Workshop was held in Drayton Valley, hosted by Brazeau County and Farming Forward. The free event helped farmers identify environmental risks and prepare for funding opportunities like AgriInvest and the Resilient Agricultural Landscape Program.
